# zmux Zig "elegance" TODOs
Idiomatic-Zig cleanup items owing to the port from tmux's idiotmatic C.
Because a fully functioning and feature-equivalent port of tmux was our
initial priority above a full port into idiomatic zig -- which would
of course entail far more refactoring than otherwise -- this file is
an attic into which porting debt is intended to collect until such time
as the port is complete and the next phase of development can begin.

That phase will of course consist of prioritizing and addressing (after
sufficient analytical chin-stroking establishes a given debt is worth
the labor cost and regression risk of resolution exceededs that of
toleration in some plausibly forseeable time horizon).

## Architecture

- Replace the transient geometry-reconstruction seam in [`src/layout.zig`](src/zmux/src/layout.zig) with persistent
  `layout_root`/`layout_cell` ownership once more split, destroy, and layout-aware consumers share one runtime.
- Decide whether to keep or remove the now-dormant UTF-8 helpers 
- Break up [`src/types.zig`](src/zmux/src/types.zig) after coverage stabilizes. It is currently a `tmux.h` replacement and coupling hub; move subsystem-owned types closer to `session.zig`, `window.zig`, `server-client.zig`, and related modules once parity work stops depending on a single umbrella types file.
- Generate or validate [`PORT-MAP.md`](/goodz/work/agents/zmux/PORT-MAP.md) and [`docs/architecture.md`](src/zmux/docs/architecture.md) from code instead of maintaining them entirely by hand. The current docs are directionally useful, but drift risk is high.
- Revisit the `@cImport` umbrella in [`src/c.zig`](src/zmux/src/c.zig). Narrow the imported C surface and prefer Zig std/os abstractions where they are already good enough.
- Move the growing keycode constant block out of [`src/types.zig`](src/zmux/src/types.zig) once the input/key-binding substrate is real. It is a defensible temporary home for the port, but it is not a good long-term ownership boundary.

The current port keeps a very C-shaped global-state model; Zig would benefit from explicit state structs with clearer ownership and lifetimes.
